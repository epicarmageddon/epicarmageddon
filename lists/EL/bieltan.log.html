Version 1.0.beta

The general changes to the Eldars, described below, makes the EA:Ragnarok Biel-Tan list differ a lot from the NetEA und Epic UK origin.

In addition to the general changes the focus on Aspect Warriors has been emphasized by limiting the number of Guardian Verbände based on the number of Aspect Verbände. We felt that the Biel-Tan army should be focused around the large und powerful Aspect Verbände, und if you want to play a Guardian based army there are other Craftworlds available.

The Biel-Tan has also received what some might consider a nerf, when the Ranger Verbände now comes in a fixed size of vier. This limits the ability of the Rangers to act as main line Verbände e.g. trigger engages.
