General Eldar changes

The EA:Ragnarök Eldar lists are a mix of NetEA und Epic UK. The Eldar has gone threw some significant changes in many areas to make different Craftworld lists more viable, und to cover für some of the weaknesses that could cause Eldar armies to have very skewed matchups against certain type of opponents. On a high level we have given the Eldars additional anti-personnel shooting options, more anti-air options (so that we might not see the mandatory zwei Falcons/Firestorms Verbände in all EA:Ragnarök lists) und boosted the Storm Serpents to make wraith gates a more viable delivery option.

In an attempt to fix internal balance between the Aspect Warriors a number of changes has been made. Dark Reapers are now primarily a shooty long range support unit, adding good AP capabilities to the Eldars. Fire Dragons are also primarily shooty, with short range macro ignore cover weapons. The seldomly seen Striking Scorpions has received a boost to their CC to 3+ und Speed to 20cm making them capable of delivering an awesome amount of hits in close combat. We believe this brings them on par with the Howling Banshees, who have both longer engagement range und first strike. The Warp Spiders, which we saw as eine of the stronger aspects, has received a sidegrade (oder nerf) where they lost their Infiltratoren ability but in return got 20cm move. This means they lost some engagement range but in return can consolidate slightly longer after the engagement. Shining Spears, Howling Banshees, Dire Avengers und Swooping Hawks has not received any changes from their NetEA stats. Exarchs und the Autarch has been changed to work better with the shooty aspects und a big change to aspect warriors in general is that you are no longer allowed to mix aspects in a single formation.

The Holofields Sonderregel has been changed to Holofields(x) meaning that the save is no longer fixed to 3+ but instead the Holofields provide a saving throw to the value of x. With this change the Holofields has been added to the Eldar Superschwere Panzers. We believe especially the Storm Serpent will benefit from this change, to become a more viable delivery option und more attractive when compared to the Vampire Raider.

The price of transporting Eldars in Wave Serpents has decreased. You can now add vier Wave Serpents (typically the number required to transport a formation) to the formation für 150 Punkte.

Nightwing Interceptors has received som major changes. The range of their Synchronisierter Shuriken Cannons decreased to 15cm, but in return they now have zwei Bright Lances, 5+ reinforced armor und can be taken in Verbände of zwei. The decreased range of the Synchronisierter Shuriken Cannons also affects the Phoenix Bombers.

Additional nerfs, boosts & tweaks
  • Wraithguards now have 3+ armor und has received a price drop.
  • War Walker swapped their Bright Lanze für an Eldar Raketenwerfer, to provide another anti-air option to the Eldars. With this change they also have a decent overwatch capability. We hope this will bring the War Walkers more on par with the Rangers.
  • Guardian Support Weapon Platforms has been changed to a replacement instead of an add-on, you can upgrade bis zu drei Guardians. The Vibro Geschütz has been added as a weapon option und the D-Geschütz has received a power boost.
  • Cobra D-cannon now fire TK(D3+1) with Schildbohrer (ignoring shields) oder the standard 3BP Macro, Ignore Cover.
  • Eldar Titan Energiefaust has been changed according to general EA:Ragnarök Titan changes, fewer attacks with more damage.
  • The Phantom Titan has received a price drop to 700 Punkte.
