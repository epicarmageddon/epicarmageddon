Version 1.0.beta

The EA:Ragnarök Ulthwe list is similar to the Epic UK list, focused around the Black Guardian squads. The Black Guardians benefit from the price drop on Wave Serpents, und in addition the option to replace Wave Serpent with Falcons und more importantly Firestorms has been added. The Black Guardians also have a 1+ Initiative Rating, making the list in general more reliable.

The Ulthwe list also has access to a new unit, the Wraith Ritters. Eldar Wraith Ritter are fast und have a 1+ activation with various weapon options making them an interesting option für triggering engages with Guardians in support.
