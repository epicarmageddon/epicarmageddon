Version 1.0.beta

The Maynarkh Dynasty Necrons is based on the NetEA und EpicUK Necron lists, but has undergone major changes due to the general Necron redesign.

The Maynarkh Dynasty Necrons has received a new formation that can garrison from any objective; the Canoptec Swarm. The Canoptec Swarm (A Canoptec Spyder und Scarabs) is a way to get presence on the battlefield from the first activation, und can be useful holding ground oder protecting teleporting portals. The list also has access to Necron Aircrafts.
