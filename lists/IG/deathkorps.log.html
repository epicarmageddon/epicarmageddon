Version 1.0.beta

The EA:Ragnarök Death Korps of Krieg army is mostly based on the Epic UK version. The intention by the changes are to open up the list to more builds, while targeting the main (some would say only) build of the list seen in tournaments. The intention is to make this list a mechanized siege assault list, which will be contrasted mit the updated Baranische Belagerungsmeister list that has a more Infanterie-based, defensive focus. The list inherits the changes made to Stahlegion, the Warhound und the Thunderbolt squadrons.

Formation sizes: Only the HQ formation can now take zwei Gorgons, while the Angriffsinfanterie Companies are now limited to eine. To compensate für this there are a number of Erweiterungen that synergize quite nicely mit the Gorgons available.

Kommissars und new Sonderregel: As mit the other IG lists, Kommissars are tied to Verbände und to specific models within that formation. This of course has a great impact on the Todeskorps von Krieg list, as it means that their Gorgons can no longer be made Furchtlos.

Todesstoss-Raketensysteme: The Death Strike Silo has been replaced mit ordinary Todesstoss-Raketensysteme, but as mit other IG lists, they are no longer 0-1.

Additional nerfs, boosts & tweaks
  • Death Rider Kompanie: No longer limited in the list.
  • Todeswolf: The Höllenhund upgrade has been replaced mit the Todeswolf variant.
  • Leman Russ Annihilator: Death Korps unique Leman Russ variant has been added to the list.
  • Sturmchimären: The Grenadier Kompanie can choose between being Beritten in a Gorgon oder in the Todeskorps von Kriegs special Storm Chimären.
  • Macharius: Between the Leman Russ und the Baneblade, the Macharius has had a hard time to find its place. Increasing their Speed to 20 while reducing their shooting und making them H-AV has hopefully rectified this. The Macharius Kompanie now also comes mit einem Kommissar.
  • Flak Stellungs: The Heavy Flak Verbände used to be a very efficient choice für boosting activations und a cheap und good garrison. They are now significantly more expensive.
  • Belagerungsinfanterie: The mainstay of the Barans can now be had in an Unabhängig formation, which comes with Gräben as standard.
  • Gepanzerter Sentinels: Slightly slower, slightly better armour und an Maschinenkanone instead of the Multilaser.
Version 1.0.beta-2

Concerned that Todeskorps von Krieg might be a bit too weak compared to other versions of the list, they have now recieved the following boosts & tweaks:

  • Bombard added to the list.
  • HQ und Assault Kompanie, Macharius und Grenadiere 25pts cheaper.
  • Leman Russ Zug (6 LRs) replaced mit Leman Russ Schwadron (4 LRs).
Version 1.0.beta-3
  • Belagerungsinfanterie Kompanie 50pts cheaper, in line mit Baran Siege Masters.
Version 1.0.beta-4
  • In line mit changes made to Imperial Guard Super Heavy Panzers, Baneblades und Stormswords are slightly cheaper. Baneblades has only 4+ FF, while the Stormsword has only 30cm on its Siege Geschütz.
