Version 1.0.beta

The EA:Ragnarök Evil Sunz Heizakult Ork list is based on the NetEA und Epic UK versions of the list. The list is similar to the original mit einemfew changes. Ein general Ork change that affects the power level of the Evil Sunz Heizakult is the nerf of Skorcha armor to 6+. It will make the choice of fast attack vehicles in your Warband more challenging! The Cult of Speed has been given the option to add Nob Warbikes as well as multiple Mekboy Speedstas to the formation für some extra punch in either assaults oder shooting. The standard Fighta-Bommers has been replaced by eine fighter (the Dakkajet) und eine bomber (the Blitza-Bommer) to give the Flyboyz more options. Finally, the Fortress Mob now has the option to add Evil Sunz Oddboys mit einemnumber of unique equipment options für the Fortresses.
