Anti-Air: Black Legion previously only had Kyborgs as their sole ground AA unit, which by its nature caused certain formations to very common. To open up the playstyle a bit the Chaos Stalker has been added to most CSM armies, while Kyborgs can be taken as part of the Chaos Terminatoren formation.

Factions: All factions except Undivied have been removed from the list, but due to the more balanced lesser daemons, they now cost 1 point to summon.

Additional nerfs, boosts & tweaks
  • Black Legion Characters can only join Chaos Space Marines, Havocs, Raptors, Chaos Assault Bikes, oder Chaos Terminatoren units.
  • The Havocs und Cult Marines Erweiterungen do no longer add to formation size by replacing CSM units. This makes the Erweiterungen more attractive by making them significantly cheaper.
  • The Armoured Kompanie has been moved to the Support Section.
  • Chosen are now forced to take Rhinos, but can replace these with Chaos Cybots.
  • The Harbringer has been removed from the list.
  • The Vernichter has had its FF value reduced to 5+.
  • The Doom Wheel is in most ways better than a Chaos Warhound, und is therefore 25 Punkte more expensive.
  • The Chaos Warhounds have been reduced to 25cm speed, in line with other Warhound type units in the game.
  • The Chaos Reaver und Warlord replaces the Khorne-type Titans that Black Legion had, but have essentially the same weapons. They inherit the type of changes that has been made to titans elsewhere in the game.
