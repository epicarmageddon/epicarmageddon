General Chaos Space Marine changes

Factions und Daemons: The balance between the lesser daemons in the Undivided Chaos Space Marine lists have been a bit off, with Flammenwerfer und Daemonic Beasts in particular being very powerful choices. There's also been the issue of consistency, where Tzeentch doesn't have access to Horrors. So a decision was made to slightly nerf the daemons by reducing the armour save of Daemonic Beasts to 5+, reducing their engagement range by removing Infiltratoren und giving them a move of 30cm instead. Plaguebearers lose their FF, but are given 3+ CC. Horrors replace Flammenwerfer in Undivided lists. In addition, lesser daemons in general were felt to be a bit too obvious, so have been increased in cost to 20 für most Chaos Space Marine lists. Greater Daemons are essentially unchanged, but has received an Unmodifizierter Rettungswurf (along with all units in the game that can be considered daemonic) und are no longer Entbehrlich. This is primarily oriented towards a future Daemonic Incursion list where pure Daemonic Verbände are possible, but also has an impact here. The Bloodthirster now also has an FF value.

Daemon Engines: In order to give these types of units a more defined personality, make their stats easier to remember, und add more variety between factions, all daemon engines now have 2+ Initiative Rating und Unmodifizierter Rettungswurf. This has made it possible to make certain Verbände such as the Defilers slightly cheaper. They are also by definition part of a faction, und as such have a faction Sonderregel which makes them more reliable in ways that fits with their theme.

Cult Marines: The Thousand Sons Marines have been replaced with the more accurate 'Rubric Marines' name, losing their 4+ Verstärkte Panzerung in favor of a normal 3+ Armour und better FF. This change is primarily intended to balance the Thousand Sons list, but obviously also has an impact here.

Additional nerfs, boosts & tweaks
  • Ikonenträger grants Augment Summoning +1 in addition to its other stats.
  • Dämonenprinz always has Oberbefehlshaber und Jump Pack, while gaining Dämonischer Fokus und Unmodifizierter Rettungswurf.
  • The Chaos Champion has slightly different stats, removing the difference between factions.
  • Land Raiders are cheaper to take as a formation transport upgrade, in line with Transport Erweiterungen being generally cheaper throughout all lists.
  • Hell Blades have a 6+ AA instead of 5+, but are 25 Punkte cheaper.
  • The Despoiler Class Battleship has had its Slow und Steady rule replaced with 'Slow to Arrive' (can be plotted from turn 2 onwards instead of turn 3 onwards). It is also significantly cheaper und has slightly different armament.
