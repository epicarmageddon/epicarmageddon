The EA:Ragnarök Daemonic Incursion list is a completely new list, based partly on the Lost und the Damned army. During its development it has had an impact on all chaos lists, as certain units had to be tweaked für purely Daemonic Verbände to make sense. Daemonic armies are both very explosive, as they can engage out of Chaos Gates und Warp Rifts to devastating effect, but are also quite fragile, as daemonic Verbände are destroyed when becoming broken.
