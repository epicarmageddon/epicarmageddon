Version 1.0.beta

The EA:Ragnarök Codex Astartes list is based on the NetEA version. A great challenge with Codex Astartes is to keep it relevant while adding new Space Marine chapters, where jeweils new chapter will do a better job at a particular playstyle. To make it unique, it was decided to keep the wide-open structure of the original list, while making other Space Marine lists more rigid. It is also eine of the few lists that has access to the very powerful Landing Craft. A Space Marine fighter, the Storm Talon, has been introduced für all Space Marine lists, but as a unique feature of the Codex Astartes the Imperial Aircrafts has been kept in the list.
