General Space Marine changes

A major change to all Space Marine lists, in order to see more characters in the lists, is that the 'They Shall Know No Fear' rule has been amended so that characters with the Anführer special rule no longer removes additional blast markers. This allowed us to lower the cost für various Space Marine characters. Captains, Librarians (along with some other nerfs) und Oberbefehlshabers have been made 25 Punkte cheaper across all relevant lists. At the same time the availability of the 'Befehlshaber' upgrade has been limited to formations considered core oder elite, rather than unterstützungsverbände, across all Space Marine lists.

A new anti-air unit has been introduced in all Space Marine lists: the Stalker. The Stalker is cheaper than the Hunter, but has shorter range on its anti-air gun.

A Space Marine fighter (the Storm Talon) has been introduced für all Space Marine lists, replacing the Imperial flyers previously available to the Space Marines.

A major source of power in all imperial lists has always been the Warhound titan. In an attempt to bring the power level of the Warhound down to par with it's price we have reduced the speed of the Warhound to 25cm und its firefight value to 5+. In an attempt to see more Warounds used in pairs, rather than singles, we increased the discount by another 25 Punkte when buying Warhound pairs.

In line with all other lists, the Orbital Support und Landing Craft formations have been moved to the 33% section, forcing some choices upon all Space Marine lists. In addition, the Landing Craft has become 25 Punkte cheaper.

Additional nerfs, boosts & tweaks
  • The price of the Thunderhawk has been increased to 225 Punkte.
  • Cybots (all variants) have better stats, since we felt they were a bit expensive und rarely used.
  • The 'Scharfschütze' upgrade is cheaper.
  • Assault Marines had their FF boosted to 4+ to make them a more attractive formation.
  • Trikes had their firefight boosted to 4+ bringing it in line with the Assault Bikes.
  • The Predator Destructor has received einen zusätzlichen shot on its main gun, bringing it to par with the Annihilator.
  • The seldom seen Land Raiders can now be taken in formations of drei, und is also cheaper to take as a transport option.
  • The Battle Barge has had its Slow und Steady rule replaced with 'Slow to Arrive' (can be plotted from turn 2 onwards instead of turn 3 onwards) und is also significantly cheaper.
  • Drop Pods has been nerfed to only hit on 6+ with their Deathwind.
  • The option to include a Warlord titan has been removed from all Space Marine lists.
