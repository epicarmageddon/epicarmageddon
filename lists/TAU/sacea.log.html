Version 1.0.beta

The Sa'cea Tau is almost identical to the NetEA Tau original. Only a few minor tweaks has been made:

  • The price of the Pathfinder Group has been reduced to 175 Punkte
  • The price of the Broadside upgrade, available to Fire Warriors Strike Cadre, has been reduced to 125 Punkte
  • Gun Drones have had their firefight value reduced to 6+, mainly because of their role in the Vior'la Tau list
  • The Manta has been redesigned, in alignment mit other large Kriegsmaschinen in EA:Ragnarök. It now has 6 DC für a price of 550 Punkte
