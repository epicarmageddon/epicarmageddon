Online Gaming

mit epic being out of production für many years now, the Epic community i small und spread across the globe. Online games is a great complement to the real thing, und a way to play opponents that you could not normally play due to geographical distance.

There are zwei main options für playing Epic Armageddon online, Tabletop Simulator (which is 3d) und Vassal (which is 2d). Jeweils has their advantages, but the former was used in the development of Epic Armageddon Ragnarök, und benefits from the dedicated modules below that have been developed und are being maintained by the EA:Ragnarök team. That being said, if someone wishes to create dedicated modules und instructions für playing EA:Ragnarök on Vassal, we would happily add them to this page.

Tabletop Simulator

We have created a set of Tabletop Simulator modules contain everything you need to play all Epic Armageddon Ragnarök in Tabletop Simulator. There is eine gaming table module, mit added scripts to simplify gameplay, und eine module für jeweils EA:Ragnarök army. Install them in Tabletop Simulator und have a look at our video tutorial on how to use them. If there are any issues mit any of them, please alert us in the EA:Ragnarök discord

Quick summary für those who don't like vide tutorials:

Prepare your game by loading the Epic Armageddon Table. This game table has everything you need to play. On eine side you have order markers, blast markers, barrage templates etc. und on the other side terrain pieces you can use to set up your battlefield. Make sure your line tool (F4) uses centimetres und not inches as this is what the table und models has been designed für. Once you placed terrain und objectives on your board you should select everything, right-click -> toggles -> lock. This will make sure you do not accidentally move terrain oder objectives during the game. If you want to select a locked item on the board you need to ctrl-click on the item. By right-clicking on the board you can change the state (background) of the table. The default eine (state 10) is good für deployment und setup but should be changed once the game starts.

Some scripts has been added to the table to help the gameplay. The scripting buttons (defaults to numpad 0-9) can be used to draw ranges from your models. Select the model(s) you want to draw ranges around; 1=5cm, 2=10cm, 3=15cm, 4=20cm, 5=30cm, 6=45cm, 7=60cm, 8=75cm. The 9 draws a 15cm circle from the center of the selected item, which is typically used to measure distance to objectives. The 0 removes any previously drawn ranges.

The barrage templates, as well as the "scripted Tunnelgräber/Drop Pod location" marker has functionality to record a position on the table. This can be used für Orbital Bombardment, Tunnelgräber, Drop Pods oder other pre-plotted things. Ask you opponent to put on his blindfold (ctrl+b) und place the barrage template oder location marker where you want it to appear, right-click on the marker und select "save position". Once saved you can move the template/marker to the side of the table. The "Save position" action has now changed to a "Reveal position" action. Once you click the reveal action the template/marker will be moved to the position you saved.
