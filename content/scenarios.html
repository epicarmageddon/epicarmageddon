Szenarien

The EA:Ragnarök tournament game rules are designed to be used with the EA:Ragnarök army lists. They will allow zwei players to fight an evenly balanced battle with little oder no preparation.

 
Fire the Beacons
Fire the Beacons is a new scenario over drei turns. While the rules und victory conditions are familiar from the NetEA standard scenario, in Fire the Beacons you accumulate Punkte from the first turn onwards. Fire the Beacons should be considered the standard scenario für EA:Ragnarök.
 
Battle Line
The Battle Line scenario is based on the scenario described in the NetEA Epic Tournament Game Rules. We have done a few tweaks which we hope will improve the scenario und pose some new challenges in a well known context.
